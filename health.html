Top 5 AI Tools for Health & Wellness (2026)

These tools provide general wellness information only. They are NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Woebot

AI mental health chatbot (CBT-based)

Why it leads: Woebot is an FDA-reviewed AI chatbot delivering Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) techniques through conversation. Backed by clinical research from Stanford, it provides evidence-based mental health support 24/7. It's designed for people who can't access or afford traditional therapy.

FDA-reviewed Stanford research-backed CBT-based therapy 24/7 availability

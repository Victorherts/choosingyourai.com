Top 5 AI Writing Tools (2026)

Data-backed rankings of the best AI writing assistants. Based on user adoption, revenue, enterprise usage, and content quality.

Last updated: May 2026

The AI writing assistant market reached $2.67 billion in 2026, growing at 21.4% CAGR. It's projected to reach $28.64 billion by 2033. 91% of marketing professionals now use AI in their workflows, up from 63% the previous year.

Why it leads: Grammarly dominates with 40 million daily active users and over 500,000 organizations using it daily. It's present in 96% of Fortune 500 companies. Enterprise adoption tripled from 17% to 47% of customer environments between 2023 and 2026. Revenue reached $700M ARR by mid-2025.

40M daily users $700M ARR 96% Fortune 500 500K+ organizations

Real-time grammar + tone + clarity
AI writing agents for rewrites
Enterprise governance features
❌ Cons
$12/month Premium
AI features limited on free tier
Less suited for long-form content
Can be overly prescriptive
Try Grammarly ↗

Data-backed rankings of the best AI tools for building resumes, beating ATS systems, and accelerating your job search.

Over 1.2 million job seekers used AI-powered job search features in 2025. 64% used AI to check ATS compatibility, while 50% used it to write resume content. LinkedIn processes 14,200 applications per minute, and 99% of Fortune 500 companies use applicant tracking systems.

Why it leads: Jobscan is trusted by over 10 million job seekers worldwide and is the most established ATS optimization tool. It compares your resume against job descriptions, highlighting missing keywords and skills. With ~$8M in estimated revenue, it's the go-to tool for beating applicant tracking systems.
